Dear lovely visitor,

Welcome to my site—I'm Kevin (he/him)! I'm a product designer on the Realm team at MongoDB, and recent graduate in Design Studies and New Media from UC Berkeley. While at Cal, I held design research positions at The BRAVO Group and the Haas School of Business, as well as co-taught a design theory/history/ethics course under the UC Berkeley Center for New Media.

Outside of design, my personal interests generally revolve around race, contemporary protests/activisms, digital culture, philosophies of technology, and minoritarian subjectivity & world-building. Understood cohesively, I'm interested in the ways digital spaces are socio-politically constructed/designed along racialized and gendered lines, yet also hold the potential to foster community, collaboration, and social movement. You can find me trying to connect the dots on my Are.na.

What I'm up to:

  • Helping curate BLM resources with some other folks, while continuously trying to sharpen my own abolitionist politic
  • Taking classes as a non-degree student at the CUNY Digital Humanities program (I'll be tracking any sporadic thoughts I have from these classes on an Are.na channel)
  • Trying to up my dev game with designcode.io
  • Getting back into writing and building the confidence to post them on this site...
